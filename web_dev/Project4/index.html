...The world’s biggest annual beatbox battle is back for it’s 11th edition, and it’s going to be bigger and better than ever! This edition of The Grand Beatbox Battle will be held for the first time in Klub Stodoła in Warsaw, Poland, from the 12th-15th April 2019. It will consist of 3 categories – Solo Showcase, Tag-Team, and Loopstation battles. The participants will be a mixture of invited beatboxers and wildcard winners. All judges and participants will be announced in the coming weeks and months. There will also be a special new show battle, which will be announced at a later date. Stay tuned, you don’t want to miss this!
